About Us

Intrascope Nigeria Limited

Intrascope Nigeria Limited is an integrated engineering company set up to provide world class services and innovative solutions for buildings in the following fields - Horizontal, Vertical and Diagonal transportation systems such as elevators, escalators,Autowalks & dumb waiters - Uninterruptible power systems ups - Automatic doors - Access control In all these specialities, we are associated with some of the leading manufacturers of the equipment around the world in order to be able to realise our goal of making cities better and more sustainable to live.

Intrascope Nigeria ltd. has rich product varieties. We can satisfy diverse requirements from various users. The products scope covers passenger elevator, observation elevator, bed elevator, freight elevator, hydraulic elevator, car elevator, small machine room elevator, machine room-less elevator, home elevator, dumbwaiter, escalator and moving walk.


The superior service guarantees the outstanding product performance. In order to satisfy the requirements from different areas, users and levels, the company has established one-continuous-line of high-efficient work system from elevator design, manufacture, installation, modernization and maintenance.
